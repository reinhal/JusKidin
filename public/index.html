Support your children. Keep all the memories.

Create profiles for each of your children. Connect to relevant web resources based on your child's age. Upload pictures you take of your child's artwork, schoolwork, sporting events and other special memories.

    How To Use JusKidin

    Web Resources for Your Children

    Parents have lots of questions on how to best support their growing children. Simply use the "Add Child" form to enter a name and birthdate for each child. Visit the Child(ren) page, click on the child to access developmentally relevant web resources.

    Organize Memories, School Work, Artwork into Drawers

    Think of drawers as topics and containers. They are simply an organization tool for your images or digitized versions of your children's work, activities and creations. Go to the Drawers page, click on the drawer button to upload images and add notes.
