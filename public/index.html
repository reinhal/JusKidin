    Here's How To Use JusKidin

    Web Resources for Your Children

  • Simple use the "Add Child" form to enter a name and birthdate for each child.
  • Visit the Child(ren) page, click on the child to see developmentally relevant web resources.

    • Organize Memories, School Work, Artwork into Drawers

  • Use the "Connect Image" form to bring the image into the website and place it into a drawer.
  • Think of drawers as topics and containers. They are simply an organization tool for your images.
  • Go to the Drawers page, click on the drawer button to view all the connected images.

    • Enjoy!
